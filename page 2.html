Overall Reception

Chef RPG has received generally positive reviews for its unique blend of cooking, exploration, and light combat. While praised for its charming art style and engaging gameplay, some players note areas for improvement such as NPC depth and quest variety. Overall, it stands out as a refreshing take on the life simulation genre.
