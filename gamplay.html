Gamplay Overveiw

Chef RPG combines exploration, ingredient gathering, hunting, and a cooking system that rewards experimentation. Players run a resturant, build recipes, and interact with townsfolk.

Core Systems

Systems What it does
CookingCombine ingredients for dishes with unique effects and customer ratings
Explorationnew ingredients, recipes, and secrets in diverse biomes
Relationships -NPCs give quests, recipes, and resturant help.

Typical Player Loop

  1. Forage, Fish, and Farm
  2. Cook dishes
  3. Serve customers & earn gold
  4. Upgrade the resturant and tools
