Key Concepts

Life Simulation
Life simulator is when a game simulate daily activities like farming, cooking, and socializing for example!
Crafting Systems
Crafting Systems combine ingredients or material to create items, dishes, or tools.
Resource Management
Balancing inventory, time, energy, and money to achieve goals.
Procedural Interaction
Events and item results based on player choices and combinations.
Player Loop
The repeating cyle of actions players take to progress (Gather -> craft -> serve -> upgrade).
Indie Development
Small team or solo practices emphasizing creativity, rapid iteration, anf community feedback.
